One more day

My work life balance now out of the military

While yes ive been out of the military nearly 10 years, i have still been trying to get my balance in the civilan world. I've picked up a few hobbies to what i thought was going to kill time as i figured it out, some of them turned into a business.


A hobby turn business was starting my own clothing brand. I was always buying pretty basic, or simple tees and hoodies. Mostly plain of a solid color maybe a clean aesthetic logo on it. I got tired of constantly spending money to brands that for the most part where "basic". Thats when i started printing my own stuff, vinyl, paint, heat press and as i continue to grow i hope to expand to a small brick stone with a few employees. Here's a link to the site Above Kulture
